Family Law

Comprehensive legal solutions for family matters and domestic relationships

What is it?

Family Law encompasses legal matters related to family relationships, including divorce, child custody, adoption, and domestic partnerships.

Who needs it?

Individuals facing family-related legal issues such as marriage dissolution, child custody disputes, adoption processes, or domestic violence situations.

How it helps

Our experienced family lawyers provide compassionate guidance, protect your rights, and work towards fair resolutions in sensitive family matters.

Process Timeline

Typically 3-6 months for uncontested cases, longer for complex disputes. Initial consultation within 24 hours of request.

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How much does it cost to use PleaNow?

PleaNow offers a transparent pricing structure. Initial consultations are free for most services. For detailed legal advice and representation, costs vary based on complexity and lawyer experience. You'll always receive upfront cost estimates before proceeding with any paid services.

How quickly will a lawyer respond to my request?

Most lawyers respond within 24-48 hours of receiving your request. For urgent matters, you can mark your request as "Urgent" to receive priority attention. Response times may vary based on the complexity of your case and lawyer availability.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, all communications through PleaNow are encrypted and protected by attorney-client privilege where applicable. We follow strict data protection protocols and never share your personal information without your consent, in compliance with Indian data protection laws.

Legal Process Questions

What documents do I need to prepare for legal consultation?

Typically, you should have: identification proof (Aadhar, PAN), address proof, any relevant contracts or agreements, correspondence related to your case, and any court notices if applicable. Our lawyers will provide a specific checklist based on your case type.

Can I get legal aid if I cannot afford a lawyer?

Yes, we work with several legal aid organizations and pro bono initiatives. Depending on your financial situation and case type, you may qualify for free legal assistance. Please mention your requirements when submitting your request.

How long do typical legal cases take to resolve?

Case duration varies: Small claims (3-6 months), Family matters (6-18 months), Civil suits (1-3 years), Criminal cases (1-5 years). Your lawyer will provide a realistic timeline based on your specific circumstances and court jurisdiction.

Lawyer & Services Questions

How are lawyers vetted on PleaNow?

All lawyers undergo rigorous verification including Bar Council registration checks, experience verification, peer reviews, and background checks. We also monitor client feedback and maintain quality standards through regular assessments.

Can I change lawyers if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, you can request a change of lawyer at any time. We have a dispute resolution process and will help you find a better match. Your case documents will be transferred securely to the new lawyer with your consent.
